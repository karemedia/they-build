Expert Alumawood Patio Covers in Wildomar, CA


If you’re looking to enhance your outdoor space in Wildomar, Alumawood patio covers offer the perfect combination of elegance, durability, and low maintenance. At They Build Aluminum, we specialize in designing and installing custom Alumawood patio covers that are built to last and styled to impress.


Whether you want a shaded area to entertain or a quiet place to relax, our patio covers are engineered to fit your needs—and your home.


What is Alumawood?


Alumawood is a high-performance aluminum material designed to mimic the warm look of wood without the hassle of maintenance. It has a wood-grain texture, but it’s actually rust-resistant, termite-proof, and won’t peel, crack, or warp.


Looks like wood. Performs like metal.


Alumawood is powder-coated, which helps it resist fading even under the Wildomar sun. It comes in a range of color options, trim styles, and design configurations—making it a top choice for homeowners who want a modern upgrade without the upkeep.


Why Choose Alumawood Over Traditional Wood?


No Rot. No Termites.


Wood is beautiful, but in Southern California’s dry summers and damp winters, it deteriorates fast. Termites love it. Moisture ruins it. Alumawood takes all the charm of wood and eliminates its weaknesses.


Long-Lasting Color and Finish


Alumawood’s baked-on finish holds up against heat, rain, and UV rays. No need for sanding, painting, or staining. Just rinse it off with a hose, and it’s good as new.


Lifetime Value


While the upfront cost of Alumawood may be slightly higher than traditional wood, the long-term savings are significant. No repairs. No repainting. Just pure enjoyment for years to come.


Custom Patio Cover Styles for Wildomar Homes


Every home in Wildomar is unique—and so is every patio we build. We offer a full line of Alumawood patio cover styles to suit any space.


Solid Roof Patio Covers


Full coverage. Full shade.


Perfect for homeowners who want maximum sun protection and shelter from the rain. A solid roof patio cover is ideal for backyard lounging, grilling, or even creating an outdoor dining room.


Lattice (Pergola-Style) Covers


Dappled light. Open feel.


Lattice covers are popular in Wildomar for their open, airy vibe. They let in light while still offering relief from direct sun. You can customize the spacing of the slats to get just the right amount of shade.


Insulated Roof Panels


Cooler space. Higher comfort.


These covers feature a foam-insulated aluminum core that blocks heat transfer, keeping your patio area noticeably cooler. They’re especially beneficial for homes with west-facing patios that get direct sun exposure in the afternoon.


Combo Patio Covers


Best of both worlds.


Want a little bit of sun and a little bit of shade? Combine solid and lattice panels into a single, hybrid design. Many Wildomar homeowners opt for this style to create multiple zones for different uses.


Built for Wildomar Weather


Wildomar’s climate calls for materials that can take the heat—and occasional seasonal rain. Alumawood patio covers are designed specifically to withstand Southern California’s temperature swings, strong UV rays, and dry conditions.


We also engineer our installations to stand up to wind, which is a common concern in the Inland Empire. All of our structures are built to code and tailored to the conditions of your specific property.
