Alumawood Patio Covers by They Build Aluminum Inc.


Blending the charming appearance of wood with the sturdy qualities of aluminum, Alumawood patio covers are a favored option for improving outdoor areas. These covers offer shade, shield from weather elements, and enhance the visual appeal of patios, decks, and gardens.


To create a patio cover that looks great and lasts a long time, it is important to choose top-notch materials and have the patio cover installed by experts. Alumawood provides the aesthetic of wood without the usual issues like decay and termite infestation.


Alumawood Explained


Alumawood patio covers are outdoor structures designed to provide shade and enhance the usability of patios. They replicate the natural appearance of wood while being fabricated from aluminum, offering a blend of aesthetic appeal and functional resilience. The core material is aluminum shaped and finished to mimic wood grain textures, producing a realistic look without the vulnerabilities associated with actual wood.


Composition of Alumawood Patio Covers


These covers are composed primarily of powder-coated aluminum components engineered to resist corrosion and fading. The manufacturing process includes applying a durable finish that sustains color vibrancy and texture over time. This construction method delivers the warmth and charm of wood combined with the low-maintenance qualities of metal.


Durability of Alumawood


What sets Alumawood apart is its exceptional ability to withstand the typical issues that often plague conventional wood patio covers:

  • Warping: Aluminum maintains its shape under changing temperatures and humidity levels, unlike wood, which is prone to bending or twisting.
  • Cracking: The flexibility and strength of aluminum prevent cracks caused by temperature changes or physical impact.
  • Rotting: Aluminum does not absorb moisture, eliminating the risk of rot prevalent in wooden structures.
  • Pest Resistance: Termites and other wood-boring insects cannot damage aluminum materials, ensuring long-term structural integrity.


In addition, alumawood patio covers withstand a variety of weather conditions, including intense sun exposure, rain, and wind, without deteriorating or requiring frequent repairs. Their robust nature makes them an excellent choice for outdoor living areas that demand both style and longevity.


Modification Options


A variety of customization choices are available with Alumawood patio covers, allowing homeowners to tailor their outdoor spaces to suit different architectural styles and personal preferences. These options enable individuals to design outdoor areas that showcase their distinct creativity and way of living.


Style Variations

  • Solid Covers: Provide full shade and protection from the elements, ideal for those seeking maximum coverage.
  • Lattice Covers: Feature an open-weave design that allows filtered sunlight, adding texture and a decorative touch.
  • Freestanding Structures: Stand independently from the house or building, perfect for creating separate outdoor living or dining areas.

Maintaining Your Alumawood and Aluminum Patio Covers


To uphold the longevity and aesthetics of your Alumawood patio covers, regular maintenance is essential. Here are some maintenance tips to keep your patio cover looking good all year:

  • Wipe down the surface with a mixture of mild soap and water to eliminate dust and debris.
  • Avoid using abrasive cleaners or tools that may scratch the wood-grain finish.
  • Inspect for any signs of wear, such as scratches or chipping, and address them promptly to prevent further damage.


By following these maintenance tips tailored to each material type, you can preserve the integrity and appearance of your patio covers for years to come. Regular care not only enhances the visual appeal of your outdoor space but also adds to the durability of these structures against environmental elements.

FAQs


What are Alumawood patio covers and why are they popular for outdoor living spaces?

Alumawood patio covers are outdoor structures made from aluminum designed to mimic the look of natural wood. They are popular in outdoor living spaces due to their durability, resistance to warping, cracking, rotting, and pests like termites, making them a long-lasting and visually appealing choice.


How durable are aluminum patio covers compared to traditional wood?

Unlike traditional wood, Alumawood patio covers resist warping, cracking, rotting, and termite damage. The aluminum composition provides superior weather resistance and longevity, ensuring your patio cover remains sturdy and attractive over time.


What customization options are available for Alumawood patio covers?

Alumawood patio covers can be customized in various designs including solid, lattice, and freestanding structures. Clients can personalize their covers with different colors, shapes, sizes, and add features such as integrated lighting or ceiling fans to enhance functionality and style.


Why is professional installation important?

Professional installation ensures safe construction and durability of Alumawood patio covers. Experts like They Build Aluminum Inc. provide a thorough process from design consultation to final construction, guaranteeing quality craftsmanship and timely project completion.
