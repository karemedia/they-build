Custom Aluminum Patio Covers for Menifee Homes

Menifee homeowners know the importance of outdoor living. With long summers and plenty of sunshine, creating a shaded space to relax, entertain, or unwind is essential. At They Build Aluminum, we specialize in custom Alumawood patio covers explicitly designed for Menifee’s climate and lifestyle. Whether you prefer a sleek, modern look or a more traditional touch, we build covers that look great and perform even better.


Why Choose Alumawood for Your Menifee Backyard?

Looks Like Wood. Performs Like Aluminum.

Alumawood is engineered to give you the best of both worlds. It’s aluminum, but it mimics the appearance of real wood without the maintenance headaches. That means no peeling, cracking, warping, or termites. Just clean, durable coverage that lasts for decades.


Built for Menifee’s Climate

Menifee’s sun can be intense. Alumawood reflects radiant heat and resists UV damage, making it ideal for Southern California backyards. It won’t fade, and it won’t get brittle over time. Rain? No problem. It’s rustproof, too.


Low Maintenance, High Impact

Forget sanding, staining, or painting. Alumawood patio covers stay beautiful with just a rinse from the hose or a wipe with a damp cloth. They free up your weekends for what matters — not yardwork.


Our Patio Cover Styles

Solid Roof Patio Covers

Need full protection from the sun and rain? A solid roof patio cover is your best bet. This style offers 100% shade and keeps outdoor furniture, grills, and flooring dry and protected year-round. It's a perfect fit for homeowners who want a seamless extension of their indoor living space.

We match the cover’s style and color to your home’s exterior. With a solid Alumawood roof, you get an insulated barrier that cools the patio beneath, helping you beat the Menifee heat.


Lattice Patio Covers

Love the sun but want just enough shade? Go for a lattice patio cover. These open-style covers filter sunlight, providing about 40–60% shade depending on the spacing of the slats.

Lattice covers are ideal for gardens, hot tubs, or poolside areas where you want a gentle breeze and some light. They create a soft, inviting ambiance that doesn't completely block out the natural elements.


Combo Patio Covers

Not sure which to choose? Do both. Our combo patio covers blend solid and lattice sections, giving you both sun and shade. These are highly customizable and popular for larger backyards or patios with multiple zones, such as a dining area on one side and a lounge space on the other.


Built by Experts with Over 25 Years of Experience

We’ve been building aluminum patio covers across Southern California for over two decades. Menifee homeowners trust us because we know what works and how to build it right.

Every project starts with a consultation. We walk your property, discuss your vision, and provide expert recommendations tailored to your space, goals, and budget. Then we handle the engineering, permits, materials, and construction.


Permit Requirements for Patio Covers in Menifee

Yes, you need a permit and yes, we take care of it. The City of Menifee requires a building permit for any attached or freestanding patio cover. But don’t worry: we know the codes, setbacks, and submission process inside and out.

Our patio covers are designed to meet or exceed local engineering requirements, including snow loads, wind loads, and fire-resistant materials. We handle everything, from structural drawings to final inspection.


Custom Options and Accessories

Colors and Textures

Alumawood is available in a wide range of colors — from subtle whites and desert sands to rich tones like mocha or sienna. You can also choose from woodgrain textures that mimic cedar, mahogany, or driftwood. We’ll help you select a finish that enhances your home’s exterior and ties the backyard together.


Decorative End Cuts

Every patio cover includes your choice of end cuts — scalloped, beveled, mitered, or corbel — to add architectural detail. It's a small detail that makes a big difference in curb appeal.


Columns and Beams

We offer a variety of beam and column styles to complement your aesthetic, ranging from classic Roman columns to sleek, modern square posts. Whether you're going for a Mediterranean, Craftsman, or contemporary style, we’ve got the design elements to bring it all together.


Integrated Lighting and Fans

Want to enjoy your space after sunset? We can integrate recessed LED lighting or add fan mounts to your patio cover. Stay cool on summer evenings and illuminate your space for gatherings that extend well into the night.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a patio cover cost in Menifee?

Prices vary depending on size, style, and accessories. On average, Alumawood patio covers range from $3,500 to $12,000. Solid covers with lighting and fans cost more than basic lattice options. We offer free estimates, so you’ll know exactly what to expect before we start.


Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with financing providers to offer flexible monthly payment plans with competitive rates. Whether you’re upgrading your home or preparing for summer, we make it easy to get started without a large upfront cost.


Can you match my existing structure?

Absolutely. We customize every detail — from colors and textures to rooflines and trim — so your patio cover looks like it belongs. We can even tie into existing eaves or roofing for a seamless connection.
