Lake Elsinore Patio Covers


Why Homeowners in Lake Elsinore Choose Alumawood


Lake Elsinore’s hot summers and sun-soaked afternoons make outdoor living a part of everyday life. That’s why Alumawood patio covers are the go-to solution for local homeowners. Alumawood combines the classic look of wood with the long-term performance of aluminum. It doesn’t rot, warp, peel, or require repainting. The textured wood-grain finish mimics real timber, but with none of the upkeep. Homeowners love the low maintenance. Install it once, enjoy it for decades.


Alumawood patio covers are also engineered to stand up to the Inland Empire’s climate extremes. Heat, UV exposure, and moisture don't wear it down. That’s crucial for homes in Lake Elsinore, where summer temperatures regularly push past 100°F. With Alumawood, your patio stays shaded, cool, and comfortable—even on the hottest days.


Solid vs Lattice Style Covers


Solid Roof Patio Covers


Need complete shade? A solid Alumawood cover is the answer. These covers provide full protection from the sun and light rain. Perfect for creating a shaded dining area, a backyard retreat, or a poolside lounge. The panels interlock tightly and feature an embossed wood texture, offering the elegance of painted wood without the hassle. Ceiling fans, recessed lighting, or infrared heaters can be added to customize your outdoor space.


Lattice Style Patio Covers


Prefer filtered light? Go with a lattice cover. The crisscross beam design allows partial sunlight while reducing UV exposure. It’s ideal for growing plants, creating breezy patios, or accenting garden walkways. The lattice spacing can be customized—from tighter patterns that block 60-70% of light, to wider gaps for an airy, open feel. Lake Elsinore residents use lattice to add charm and character without enclosing the outdoors.


Combination Covers


Can’t decide? Choose a combo cover. Combine solid and lattice sections to create multi-zone outdoor spaces. Solid for the dining area. Lattice over the grill station. It’s the best of both worlds. And with They Build Aluminum, you can fully customize the layout to fit your backyard’s unique dimensions and sun exposure.


Customization Options for Lake Elsinore Homes


No two homes are the same, and your patio cover shouldn’t be either. Alumawood comes in multiple colors—White, Desert Sand, Spanish Brown, Latte, and more. Whether your home is modern, Mediterranean, or Spanish revival, there's a shade to match. Choose from decorative post caps, end cuts, and column wraps to elevate the design. Add lighting, ceiling fans, and even gutters for a truly custom setup.


You can also choose the beam size, rafter spacing, and structural layout. Want a freestanding patio? A wrap-around cover that blends into the roofline? Covered walkways? All of it is possible. Our team handles layout planning, elevation considerations, and drainage. Every cover is measured, fabricated, and installed for your specific property.


Energy Efficiency and Comfort


Alumawood isn’t just about aesthetics—it enhances comfort. By shading south- and west-facing windows, you reduce solar heat gain. That means your home stays cooler during Lake Elsinore’s hottest months. Lower indoor temperatures equal lower AC bills. Some clients report 10-15% reductions in summer energy costs after installation.


Solid patio covers also reduce sun exposure on decks, doors, and outdoor furniture. That prolongs the life of finishes and prevents fading. Lattice covers offer protection for delicate plants while letting in enough sun for healthy growth. It’s a win-win for homeowners who want beauty and function.


Durability in Southern California Conditions


Lake Elsinore’s climate can be unforgiving—scorching sun, dry heat, and occasional wind gusts. Alumawood is engineered to withstand it all. It features a high-performance Teflon®-coated polyester paint system baked into the aluminum. This finish resists corrosion, dirt, chalking, and fading. The aluminum itself is extruded and reinforced, making it much stronger than vinyl or plastic.


Unlike wood, Alumawood won’t absorb water. It’s impervious to termites, dry rot, and mold. That makes it ideal for poolside installations or humid conditions. The product is backed by a limited lifetime warranty, so you're covered for the long haul.


Permit and HOA Compliance in Lake Elsinore


They Build Aluminum handles all permit and code requirements for Lake Elsinore. Our team is familiar with local regulations and works directly with the city to ensure your patio cover meets all safety and zoning codes. We also assist with HOA approvals and provide documentation, drawings, and product details as needed.


Every project is built to Title 24 energy standards and includes proper engineering load calculations. From site inspection to final inspection, we ensure full compliance and peace of mind.


Installation Process from Start to Finish


Step 1: Free On-Site Consultation

We begin with a visit to your home. Our design experts review your space, measure dimensions, assess sun angles, and discuss your vision. You’ll see material samples and get personalized recommendations.


Step 2: Design & Permit Planning

Next, we create a custom layout with your chosen style, features, and accessories. We prepare drawings, submit permits, and handle all paperwork. No stress, no surprises.


Step 3: Precision Installation

Once approved, we schedule the installation. Our experienced crews complete most projects in 1–3 days, depending on complexity. Every cover is anchored to code, with clean edges, perfect symmetry, and attention to detail.


Step 4: Final Inspection & Walkthrough

We walk you through the finished project, check every detail, and ensure you’re fully satisfied. The result? A beautiful, functional patio cover built to last for decades.
